# Equipment

In Rune Hero, equipment plays a crucial role in enhancing a character's combat prowess. The game offers a deep and comprehensive equipment system, where players can acquire and upgrade gear to build powerful characters. The variety and customization options in the equipment system bring endless strategic possibilities and challenges.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/UI_Equipment (1).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>UI Equipment</p></figcaption></figure>

### Equipment Variety

Equipment in Rune Hero is divided into several categories, including weapons, armor, accessories, and special gear. Each category comes in different rarities, ranging from common to legendary, with every piece of equipment offering unique attributes and effects. Players can obtain equipment by defeating powerful enemies, completing dungeon challenges, and exploring the game world. Legendary equipment not only has superior base stats but also features special passive effects, significantly boosting a character’s combat abilities.

### Randomization and Enhancement

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/UI_Enhance.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Enhance Equipment</p></figcaption></figure>

To increase personalization and uniqueness, Rune Hero incorporates a random attribute system. Every piece of equipment generates random attributes when dropped, such as boosting attack power, increasing defense, or enhancing specific skill effects. Players can optimize their gear by selecting equipment that best suits their character, creating the most efficient combinations to maximize overall combat power.

In addition, players can further upgrade their gear through the enhancement and progression systems. By investing resources, players can enhance their equipment to increase its base stats. Alternatively, equipment progression allows players to upgrade the item’s rarity level, unlocking more powerful bonus effects. These systems make the process of acquiring and upgrading gear a long-term growth experience, providing players with continuous challenges and motivation.

### Equipment Set

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/EquipmentSuit.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Equipment Set</p></figcaption></figure>

The set system is an integral part of Rune Hero’s equipment mechanics. Certain gear pieces belong to the same set, and equipping multiple items from the same set allows players to unlock additional set bonuses. These bonuses can range from increasing base stats to enhancing specific skill effects and even granting unique passive abilities. The more complete the set, the stronger the activated bonuses. When players collect a full set, they gain a massive boost in power, giving them a significant edge in combat.

The set system introduces more strategic options for players, allowing them to adjust their gear combinations based on combat needs and unlock the bonuses that best complement their playstyle. This flexibility in gear configuration not only enhances character customization but also provides players with long-term goals to pursue.

### Equipment Acquisition

In Rune Hero, there are multiple ways to obtain gear. Players can acquire rare equipment by defeating dungeon bosses, completing quests, or participating in PvP arenas. Legendary gear, however, requires players to face strong enemies or challenges in high-difficulty dungeons or PvP battles to gather materials for forging. This acquisition system injects a strong sense of accomplishment into the game, as each piece of powerful equipment represents the player’s effort and strategy.
