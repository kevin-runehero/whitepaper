# Characters

In Rune Hero, players have the opportunity to take on the roles of various unique heroes, each equipped with exclusive skills, a talent system, and a powerful Ultimate ability. These elements provide a rich array of strategic options and an immersive gameplay experience. Whether you prefer to charge into battle on the front lines or provide support from the rear, Rune Hero offers a character that fits your playstyle.

### Character and Skill Diversity

Each hero in Rune Hero comes with at least 10 unique skills, each designed to offer strategic advantages in different combat scenarios. Players must choose and deploy these skills wisely, adapting to the demands of each battle and aligning with their preferred gameplay style to gain the upper hand.

### Talent System and Build Customization

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/UI_Skill.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>UI Skill</p></figcaption></figure>

Character progression in Rune Hero is not just about leveling up; it’s deeply rooted in a complex and intricate talent system. Every skill is accompanied by 3 enhancement talents and 3 mutation talents, from which players can activate one of each, significantly altering the skill’s effects. Additionally, each skill comes with 2 bonus talents that unlock when all 3 enhancement or mutation talents reach level 4 or higher, further amplifying the skill's power. This flexible talent system allows players to craft unique builds tailored to their strategies and combat needs, greatly enhancing the game’s strategic depth.

### Ultimate Skills

{% embed url="https://files.gitbook.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F0cgzQNJpMhVFzJKCx9j7%2Fuploads%2FRmwRvZSlhNhvmLZUDAQl%2FReks_Ulti.mp4?alt=media&token=c9331058-ef53-49b1-95ce-08d21c7e33a6" %}

Each class possesses a powerful Ultimate skill, which serves as one of the core abilities of the character, embodying the essence of their role and turning the tide of battle at crucial moments. For example, the Berserker Reks’s Ultimate, Exorcism, channels his full strength to unleash devastating damage on enemies, breaking through all evil forces and gaining an instant advantage in combat. The strategic use of these Ultimate abilities not only tests a player's timing but also serves as a critical factor in determining the outcome of battles.

### Classes and Their Specialties

Currently, Rune Hero features multiple classes, each with a distinctive combat style and skill tree:

**Elemental Mage - Aelia** A long-range damage dealer and control class specializing in manipulating fire, frost, and lightning. Aelia’s skill tree offers various development paths, allowing players to build for burst damage or control capabilities through different talent choices.

**Berserker - Reks** A warrior known for his melee burst and survivability. Reks’s skill tree enables players to choose between high damage output and tank-like defense, making him versatile in fulfilling multiple combat roles.

**Witch Doctor - Morakus** A mysterious character who excels in toxins and healing. Morakus’s skill tree allows players to switch between sustained damage and support roles, making him an indispensable asset to any team.

To continually expand the game’s content, we plan to introduce more classes in future updates. These new classes will bring a wider array of skill combinations and talent options, further enhancing Rune Hero’s strategic depth and gameplay diversity, providing players with even more challenges and enjoyment.
