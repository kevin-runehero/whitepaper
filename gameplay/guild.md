# Guild

In Rune Hero, the guild system is more than just a platform for players to communicate and collaborate; it serves as a vital hub that connects various game systems. By fostering collective growth and resource sharing, guilds provide members with numerous conveniences and support, enabling players to maximize their team potential in both PvE and PvP activities, while enhancing individual and group combat abilities.

### The Connecting Role of Guilds

Guilds in Rune Hero play a pivotal role in linking multiple game systems. Whether it’s dungeon challenges, battlefield confrontations, or resource acquisition and progression, guilds provide strong support for their members. Joining a guild not only gives players a reliable team but also grants access to resources and bonuses that boost efficiency and combat power across various aspects of the game.

Through guilds, players can easily form teams for dungeon challenges, participate in exclusive guild events, and grow alongside other members. Guilds also offer a space for social interaction and collaboration, making team play more efficient and cohesive.

### Guild Development Focus

The direction in which a guild develops is a key factor in attracting members. Each guild can choose its development goals, focusing on enhancing specific capabilities. For example:

* **PvE-Oriented Guilds**: These guilds typically invest resources into abilities related to dungeon challenges, such as improving drop rates or boosting members’ combat effectiveness in PvE. The goal of PvE guilds is to maximize efficiency in dungeon runs and help members acquire more resources and rewards during these challenges.
* **PvP-Oriented Guilds**: In contrast, PvP guilds focus on strengthening their prowess in battlefield combat. These guilds may choose to enhance members’ attack and defense abilities or improve tactical effectiveness in PvP arenas. PvP guilds often excel in battles and other competitive activities, leading their members to victory in confrontations.

The different development paths of guilds offer players a variety of choices. Members can select the guild that best suits their playstyle and goals, allowing them to participate more effectively in either PvE or PvP activities.

### End-of-Season Guild Rewards

At the end of each season, guilds are evaluated based on rankings, activity levels, and other performance metrics. Top-performing guilds will receive generous rewards, reflecting the guild’s overall strength and providing members with a sense of pride. Rewards may include rare resources, exclusive gear, or special titles, offering both tangible and symbolic recognition for the guild’s achievements.
