# Raid

In Rune Hero, Raids are team-based dungeons where players can group together to embark on challenging adventures. Through teamwork and tactical coordination, players will face tougher enemies and quests, exploring meticulously designed levels while striving to obtain rare loot.

### Teamwork and Adventure

Raid dungeons are designed specifically for group adventures, allowing players to form teams and confront more formidable enemies. Team cooperation is crucial in Raids, where players must have clearly defined roles. Tanks focus on drawing enemy attention, healers maintain the team's health, and damage dealers concentrate their firepower to bring down foes. Only through effective team coordination can players emerge victorious in these high-difficulty dungeons.

### Unique Environments and Powerful Bosses

Each Raid dungeon is a carefully crafted adventure, featuring unique environments and monster configurations. Players will traverse diverse terrains and encounter distinctive enemies, with Bosses being especially powerful. These Raid Bosses often come with complex abilities and phase-based mechanics that cannot be overcome by individual effort alone. Teams must utilize strong tactical planning and communication to successfully complete these challenges.

### Rare Drops and Unique Rewards

Compared to single-player dungeons, Raid dungeons offer rarer and more valuable drops, with some equipment and items only obtainable through Raids. These rare rewards not only significantly enhance a character's strength but also provide players with a sense of achievement. By defeating Raid Bosses, players have the chance to acquire legendary equipment, set pieces, and other powerful items, further boosting their combat capabilities.
