# Tournament

The Tournament in Rune Hero is a competitive feature designed specifically for casual players, focusing on quick duels and instant feedback. It allows all players to participate easily, leveraging their game knowledge and skill execution to earn rewards and a sense of accomplishment in a short amount of time.

### Unique Rules and Gameplay

Each Tournament has its own unique set of rules, gameplay, and reward systems. Different Tournaments may require specific conditions to participate, and players must adapt their strategies to meet the unique requirements of each competition. Whether it's limited skill usage, random maps, or specific match objectives, every Tournament offers a fresh challenge and a new layer of fun.

### Open Participation and Reward System

All players are free to join Tournaments, regardless of their character level or gear. The results of each match are evaluated based on player performance, with winners who meet the reward criteria receiving substantial prizes. Rewards are not limited to in-game items and resources; players also have the opportunity to win cash prizes, making Tournaments an ideal path for players to pursue both honor and resources.

### Fast-Paced and Immediate Feedback

Tournaments are a fast-paced competitive mode that provides immediate feedback and rewards. Players can participate in multiple matches within a short timeframe, quickly assessing their in-game performance and adjusting their tactics and strategies based on feedback. Each match outcome directly influences the player's rewards, allowing them to enjoy the competition while instantly seeing the fruits of their efforts.
