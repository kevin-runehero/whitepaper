# Outland(PvEvP)

**Outland** is a high-risk, high-reward open-world PvEvP map where players can hunt monsters, gather resources, and engage in player combat. Only those who survive and successfully extract with their loot will truly claim their rewards.

### Core Gameplay

* **Solo or Team Entry**\
  Players may choose to enter alone for stealth and agility, or with teammates for safety and efficiency.
* **Fierce Monsters & Aetherite**\
  Dangerous monsters roam the map. Defeating them has a chance to yield a random amount of **Aetherite**.
* **Aetherite Veins**\
  Aetherite veins spawn randomly across the map. Players can mine these veins to obtain Aetherite of varying quality.
* **PvP Encounters & Loot Drops**\
  While exploring, players may encounter others. In Outland, PvP is fully enabled.
  * Upon death, a player will **drop a random portion** of the Aetherite, equipment, and currency from their inventory.
  * Dropped items remain in the field and can be freely looted by others.
*   **Extraction & Securing Loot**\
    To keep their spoils, players must successfully exit Outland.

    * Once extracted, all items obtained will be permanently secured in the player’s inventory.
    * Aetherite can be sold to NPCs for valuable rewards.



### Risk vs. Reward

Outland is designed to create tension and player-driven conflict:

* The more a player gathers, the greater the potential reward—and the greater the risk of losing it.
* Successful extractions provide significant returns, while failure may result in heavy losses.
* This dynamic ensures ongoing competition and interaction between PvE and PvP-oriented players.
