# xRune: The Genesis NFT

The design of xRune is centered around incentivizing long-term players, and it plays a crucial role within the entire ecosystem of Rune Hero. This unique NFT comes with a dedicated xRune system in the game, allowing players to stake their xRune holdings and activate corresponding benefits throughout each season. The seasonal benefits are tiered based on the quantity of xRune staked, offering players varying levels of rewards.

### **Benefits of xRune:**

1. **Early Access:** Players with this privilege can start their gameplay early at the beginning of each season, gaining a significant advantage in resources and time.
2. **Extra Arcane Crystal Slots:** Players can unlock up to five slots for equipping Arcane Crystals, with the fifth slot accessible only after activating xRune, enhancing efficiency and returns from dungeons.
3. **Transaction Rebates:** Players will receive a portion of rebates on transactions made in the game after activating their xRune.
4. **Ongoing Empowerment:** As the game progresses, we will continuously add more empowering features to the xRune rights mechanism, enhancing its value.

### **Sales Details:**

* **Total Supply:** 3,600
* **Sales Rules:** xRune will be sold in phases, with the first batch consisting of 1,200 units. After this initial sale, subsequent rounds will be determined based on actual market demand during game development. Several conditions must be met to initiate a new sales round:
  1. Each new round's issuance price must be no less than double the previous round's price but below the current market floor price.
  2. The number of units sold in each round cannot exceed the number sold in the previous round.
